Where Code Meets Curiosity

My story starts in the bustling streets of Karachi, where the honking rickshaws and vibrant markets taught me one thing early on: adaptability is survival. But while the city thrived on chaos, I found my rhythm in patterns—lines of code, data points, and the quiet thrill of solving puzzles that others called "impossible."

The Python Wizard

Python became my wand. Mornings were for casting spells: building Reddit bots that turned scattered usernames into organized marketing tribes (picture a digital shepherd corralling users across subreddits). Clients called it "magic." I called it coffee fuel.

By night, I'd morph into a business whisperer for XYZies Pakistan, untangling customer needs under the glow of screens. "Which internet plan suits you?" turned into chess matches of upselling, while my code from the morning hummed in the background, scraping data or automating Discord servers. One moment I'm explaining fiber-optic perks to a night owl client; the next, I'm tweaking a bot to send cat memes on demand. Balance? I reinvented it.

Business Graduate with a Machine's Mind

But here's the twist: I'm a business graduate who speaks machine. While classmates memorized SWOT analyses, I taught Python to Excel sheets, making them dance to my formulas. At KMH Solutions, I bridged two worlds—using code to automate grunt work while charming clients with the art of the deal. Closed 20+ contracts? Easy. The real win was proving that spreadsheets and storytelling could coexist.

The Data Alchemist's Toolkit

Today, I'm somewhere between a data alchemist and a startup Swiss Army knife. My toolkit?

  • Python spells to automate the boring stuff
  • Web scrapers that mine gold from digital mountains
  • A business brain that turns numbers into narratives

Beyond the Code

When I'm not dissecting data on Tableau or cleaning datasets like a digital Marie Kondo, you'll find me obsessing over Karachi's street food scene (pro tip: seek the samosas sold near Urdu Bazaar). Fluent in Urdu, conversational in Python, and currently decoding the language of neural networks.

Why Work With Me?

Because I don't just build bots—I build bridges. Between daylight code and midnight hustle. Between raw data and "Aha!" moments. Between "What if?" and "What's next?"

Let's turn your chaos into code.
